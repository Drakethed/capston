A little history of myself.

Growing up I was always over weight. I could never keep
the weight off for long periods of time. It wasn't until I
joined the military that I understood what I was doing wrong.

The military taught me that not only did I Have to eat well,
I had to exercise to stay the way I wanted to be.
This may be hard to understand for someone that thinks exercise is the
endall when they want to be healthy.

On this website you will learn how to be healthy physically.
The other two parts of the triangle is something that cant not be
taught and thats something you will need to discover for yourself.
I have found that when you're happy with yourself, the others come
easier than you could think.
